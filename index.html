Mirror — Signal — Manoeuvre. Run this before every move and you'll already drive better than half the road.

Chapter 05

Nail the parallel park

The manoeuvre everyone fears. Press play and watch the line — then read the beats.

🚙
🚙
🚗
SPACE
  1. Pull up level with the parked car, ~1m gap.
  2. Reverse slowly; full lock toward the kerb as your back hits their bumper.
  3. When at ~45°, straighten the wheels and keep reversing.
  4. Full lock the other way to swing the front in.
  5. Straighten up, centre in the space. Done. 😎

Chapter 06

Think like a hazard-spotter

Good drivers aren't faster — they see sooner. Build these instincts.

👀

Scan & plan

Keep your eyes moving — far ahead, mirrors, sides. Always have an escape route.

🚸

Expect the unexpected

Kids, cyclists, doors opening. Assume the worst near schools, junctions and parked cars.

↔️

Keep space

Two-second gap — double it in rain, multiply in ice. Space buys you time.

🌧️

Read conditions

Slow for rain, fog, sun glare and worn surfaces. Smooth inputs win.
